Collection of orthologous groups as individual fasta files as defined by
members of the Snel lab.
Kinetochore by Jolien and Eelco.
IFT proteins by John.
TAF proteins by Berend and Jeffrey
SKA DAM NDC by Jolien
The sequences have been mapped against eukarya 4.0.2 by checksum and
best blast hits as per map_sequence_id.py.
